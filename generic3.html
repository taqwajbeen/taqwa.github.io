Global Startups Data Analysis


Introduction

Welcome to this dedicated segment of my portfolio, where I'm thrilled to present an engaging project that underscores the synergy between SQL and Tableau in the dynamic world of global startups. Through a comprehensive exploration of startup data, this project unveils invaluable insights that define the entrepreneurial landscape across the globe.


Embarking on the Startup Exploration:

Venturing into the realm of startups, this project serves as a testament to the power of data-driven insights. By harnessing the combined potential of SQL and Tableau, I embarked on a journey to uncover trends, patterns, and correlations that shape the trajectory of startups on a global scale.


Revealing Insights with SQL

At the heart of this exploration is SQL, a powerful tool for data manipulation and analysis. Armed with its querying capabilities, I navigated through extensive startup datasets to extract meaningful information. From funding rounds to industry trends, SQL empowered me to unravel insights that paint a comprehensive picture of the startup ecosystem.


Visualizing Startup Dynamics with Tableau

Yet, numbers alone can often lack the impact of visual representation. This is where Tableau steps in, offering an innovative canvas for visual storytelling. By crafting interactive dashboards and compelling charts, I transformed raw data into coherent narratives. These visualizations allow viewers to grasp the intricate dynamics of startups worldwide, enhancing our understanding of this vibrant landscape.
