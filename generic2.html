Welcome to this dedicated section of my portfolio, where I'm excited to present an insightful project that showcases the synergy between SQL and Tableau in the realm of healthcare analysis. Through meticulous exploration of Medicare data, this project aims to shed light on critical insights within the healthcare landscape.


Exploring Medicare Insights

Delving into the intricacies of healthcare data, this project serves as a testament to the power of data-driven decision-making. By harnessing the capabilities of SQL and Tableau, I embarked on a journey to uncover trends, patterns, and correlations that define the Medicare landscape in the United States.


Unlocking Insights with SQL

At the core of this exploration lies SQL, a potent tool for data manipulation and analysis. Armed with the ability to query and transform complex datasets, I navigated through the Medicare data to extract meaningful information. From provider demographics to patient diagnoses, SQL allowed me to unravel insights that provide a comprehensive understanding of the healthcare ecosystem.


Visualizing Healthcare Landscapes with Tableau

Yet, numbers alone can sometimes fall short in conveying the full impact of data. This is where Tableau steps in, offering an immersive canvas for visual storytelling. With Tableau's array of interactive dashboards and insightful charts, I transformed data into meaningful narratives. These visualizations enable viewers to comprehend complex healthcare trends effortlessly, enhancing our understanding of the intricate world of Medicare.
